EMPLOYEE BENEFITS OVERVIEW
Document ID: HR-BEN-2024-002
Effective: January 1, 2024

1. HEALTH INSURANCE

1.1 Medical Coverage
TechCorp offers three medical plan options:
- Basic Plan: $150/month employee contribution, $2,000 deductible, 80/20 coinsurance
- Standard Plan: $300/month employee contribution, $1,000 deductible, 90/10 coinsurance
- Premium Plan: $450/month employee contribution, $500 deductible, 100% coverage after deductible

All plans include: preventive care (100% covered), mental health services, prescription drug coverage, and telemedicine. Dependents can be added at additional cost.

1.2 Dental Coverage
- Preventive (cleanings, x-rays): 100% covered
- Basic (fillings, extractions): 80% covered
- Major (crowns, root canals): 50% covered
- Annual maximum: $2,500 per person
- Orthodontics: 50% covered up to $3,000 lifetime max

1.3 Vision Coverage
- Annual eye exam: 100% covered
- Frames: $200 allowance every 24 months
- Contact lenses: $150 allowance annually
- Laser eye surgery: 15% discount through partner providers

2. RETIREMENT BENEFITS

2.1 401(k) Plan
- Company matches 100% of employee contributions up to 4% of salary
- Additional 50% match on contributions between 4-6% of salary
- Immediate vesting on employee contributions
- Company match vests over 3 years (33% per year)
- Both traditional and Roth 401(k) options available

2.2 Stock Options
- Granted based on level and performance
- 4-year vesting schedule with 1-year cliff
- Exercise window: 90 days after departure (180 days for 5+ year employees)

3. WELLNESS BENEFITS

3.1 Gym Membership
- Up to $100/month reimbursement for gym or fitness class memberships
- On-site gym available at headquarters (free for all HQ employees)

3.2 Mental Health
- 12 free therapy sessions per year through BetterHelp partnership
- Employee Assistance Program (EAP): 24/7 counseling hotline
- Mental health days: 2 additional days per year (no documentation required)

3.3 Wellness Stipend
- $500 annual wellness stipend for: ergonomic equipment, standing desks, health apps, fitness trackers, nutrition programs

4. ADDITIONAL BENEFITS

4.1 Life Insurance
- Company provides 2x annual salary basic life insurance at no cost
- Optional supplemental life insurance available up to 5x salary

4.2 Disability Insurance
- Short-term disability: 60% of salary for up to 26 weeks
- Long-term disability: 60% of salary after 26 weeks

4.3 Commuter Benefits
- Pre-tax commuter spending account up to $300/month
- $50/month cycling benefit for bike commuters
- Free parking at company offices (where available)

4.4 Education Assistance
- Tuition reimbursement up to $10,000/year for job-related degrees
- Professional certification reimbursement (100% for first attempt)
- $2,000 annual professional development budget (separate from tuition)

4.5 Employee Discounts
- 20% discount on company products
- Corporate discount programs with: Apple, Dell, AT&T, Verizon, local gyms, and travel partners
