PERFORMANCE REVIEW AND APPRAISAL PROCESS
Document ID: HR-POL-2024-007
Effective: January 2024

1. OVERVIEW

TechCorp conducts performance reviews on a semi-annual basis: mid-year review (July) and annual review (December). The performance management cycle is designed to align individual goals with company objectives.

2. PERFORMANCE RATING SCALE

Ratings are on a 1-5 scale:
- 5 (Exceptional): Consistently exceeds all expectations. Top 5% of performers.
- 4 (Exceeds Expectations): Frequently exceeds expectations. Top 20% of performers.
- 3 (Meets Expectations): Consistently meets all job requirements. Standard performance.
- 2 (Needs Improvement): Occasionally falls short of expectations. Requires development plan.
- 1 (Unsatisfactory): Consistently fails to meet expectations. Requires immediate improvement plan (PIP).

3. REVIEW PROCESS

3.1 Self-Assessment
Employees complete a self-assessment form covering: key achievements, areas for improvement, goals for next period, and training needs. This must be submitted 2 weeks before the review meeting.

3.2 Manager Assessment
Managers complete their assessment independently, considering: quality of work, productivity, teamwork, initiative, and adherence to company values. 360-degree feedback is collected for employees at Level 4 and above.

3.3 Calibration
Department heads meet to calibrate ratings across teams to ensure consistency and fairness. The calibration committee consists of all VPs and the CHRO.

3.4 Review Meeting
A 1-hour meeting between the employee and manager to discuss ratings, feedback, and goals. HR may attend for employees rated 2 or below.

4. PROMOTION CRITERIA

4.1 Eligibility
- Minimum 18 months in current role
- Rating of 4 or above in the last two consecutive reviews
- Completion of required training for the target level
- Manager nomination and VP approval

4.2 Promotion Cycle
Promotions are processed twice per year: April and October. Out-of-cycle promotions require SVP approval and are limited to exceptional circumstances.

5. COMPENSATION ADJUSTMENTS

5.1 Annual Merit Increase
- Rating 5: 12-15% increase
- Rating 4: 8-10% increase
- Rating 3: 4-6% increase
- Rating 2: 0-2% increase
- Rating 1: No increase (PIP initiated)

5.2 Market Adjustment
In addition to merit increases, TechCorp conducts annual market benchmarking. Employees significantly below market rate may receive an additional adjustment of up to 10%.
