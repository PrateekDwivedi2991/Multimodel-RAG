LEAVE AND TIME-OFF POLICY
Document ID: HR-POL-2024-003
Last Updated: March 15, 2024

1. ANNUAL LEAVE (PAID TIME OFF)

1.1 Entitlement
- Employees with 0-2 years of service: 18 days per year
- Employees with 2-5 years of service: 22 days per year  
- Employees with 5-10 years of service: 26 days per year
- Employees with 10+ years of service: 30 days per year

1.2 Accrual
Leave accrues monthly on a pro-rata basis. Maximum carryover to the next calendar year is 5 days. Any excess leave not used by March 31 of the following year will be forfeited.

1.3 Requesting Leave
All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 5 business days in advance. Leave exceeding 5 consecutive days requires manager approval. Leave exceeding 10 consecutive days requires both manager and HR approval.

2. SICK LEAVE

2.1 Entitlement
All employees receive 12 sick days per year. Sick leave does not accrue and resets on January 1 each year.

2.2 Documentation
For sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days, a medical certificate is required. The certificate must be submitted to HR within 2 business days of returning to work.

3. MATERNITY AND PATERNITY LEAVE

3.1 Maternity Leave
Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of paid maternity leave at full salary. An additional 8 weeks of unpaid leave may be taken. Maternity leave must begin no earlier than 4 weeks before the expected due date.

3.2 Paternity Leave
Secondary caregivers are entitled to 4 weeks of paid paternity leave at full salary. This must be taken within 6 months of the child's birth or adoption.

4. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
- Immediate family (spouse, children, parents): 5 days paid
- Extended family (siblings, grandparents, in-laws): 3 days paid
- Close friends or other relatives: 1 day paid

5. PUBLIC HOLIDAYS
TechCorp observes 11 public holidays per year. If a public holiday falls on a weekend, the nearest weekday is observed. Employees required to work on public holidays receive double pay.
