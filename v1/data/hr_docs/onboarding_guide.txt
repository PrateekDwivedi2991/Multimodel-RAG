NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE
Document ID: HR-OPS-2024-005
Last Updated: April 2024

1. PRE-BOARDING (Before Day 1)

1.1 IT Setup
- Laptop provisioning request submitted 5 business days before start date
- Email account and Slack workspace access created
- VPN credentials and security tokens issued
- Required software licenses allocated

1.2 Paperwork
- Employment contract signed via DocuSign
- Tax forms (W-4, I-9) completed
- Direct deposit enrollment
- Emergency contact information
- Benefits enrollment package sent

2. WEEK 1: ORIENTATION

Day 1 (Monday):
- Welcome breakfast with team (9:00 AM)
- HR orientation session (10:00 AM - 12:00 PM): Company overview, policies, benefits
- Lunch with buddy/mentor (12:00 PM)
- IT setup and security training (2:00 PM - 4:00 PM)
- Meet with manager: 30/60/90 day plan discussion (4:00 PM)

Day 2-3:
- Department-specific orientation
- Product/service deep-dive sessions
- Introduction to key stakeholders
- Access to internal tools and documentation

Day 4-5:
- Shadow experienced team members
- Complete mandatory compliance training modules
- Set up development environment (engineering roles)

3. BUDDY PROGRAM

Every new employee is assigned a buddy from a different team. The buddy provides:
- Informal guidance on company culture and norms
- Lunch buddy for the first 2 weeks
- Monthly check-ins for the first 3 months
- The buddy is NOT a substitute for the manager or HR

4. REQUIRED TRAINING (First 30 Days)

All employees must complete:
- Information Security Awareness (2 hours, online)
- Anti-Harassment Training (1 hour, online)
- Data Privacy and GDPR Compliance (1.5 hours, online)
- Company Values Workshop (3 hours, in-person)
- Fire Safety and Emergency Procedures (30 minutes, in-person)

5. 30/60/90 DAY MILESTONES

30 Days:
- Complete all mandatory training
- Understand team structure and key stakeholders
- Begin contributing to assigned project
- First 1:1 review with manager

60 Days:
- Independently handle routine tasks
- Contribute meaningfully to team meetings
- Complete at least one project deliverable
- Second 1:1 review with manager

90 Days:
- End of probationary period review
- Fully ramped and productive
- Benefits eligibility confirmed
- Performance goals set for remainder of year
