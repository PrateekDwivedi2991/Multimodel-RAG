EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK - Version 3.2
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Company: TechCorp Industries

CHAPTER 1: WELCOME AND COMPANY OVERVIEW

Welcome to TechCorp Industries. This handbook outlines the policies, procedures, and benefits available to all employees. TechCorp was founded in 2005 and has grown to over 2,500 employees across 5 offices globally.

Our mission is to deliver innovative technology solutions that empower businesses worldwide. We value integrity, collaboration, innovation, and customer focus.

CHAPTER 2: EMPLOYMENT POLICIES

2.1 Equal Employment Opportunity
TechCorp is an equal opportunity employer. We do not discriminate based on race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, national origin, age, disability, or veteran status.

2.2 At-Will Employment
Employment at TechCorp is at-will, meaning either the employee or the company may terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause or notice.

2.3 Probationary Period
All new employees undergo a 90-day probationary period. During this time, performance is closely monitored and feedback is provided bi-weekly. Benefits eligibility begins after successful completion of the probationary period.

2.4 Work Hours
Standard work hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM with a 1-hour lunch break. Flexible work arrangements may be available with manager approval. Core hours when all employees must be available are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

2.5 Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Fully remote arrangements require VP-level approval. Remote employees must maintain a dedicated workspace and reliable internet connection with minimum 50 Mbps download speed.
